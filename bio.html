INTRODUCING KONSTANTIN BUKOV, M.D.

Perhaps the first thing you’ll notice about Dr. Konstantin Bukov is his attentiveness. Without judgment or criticism, without making you self conscious, he turns all his attention on you and he honors the uniqueness of what he sees in you.

To ensure that each treatment is personalized and carefully administered, Dr. Bukov will individually meet with you on your first visit, to hear about your concerns, and to address any issues you may have. Together, you and Dr. Bukov will construct a plan to help you achieve your goals.

FOCUS ON ANTI-AGING AESTHETIC MEDICINE

Dr. Bukov focuses on your total health and wellness. He offers anti-aging medical treatments that improve sleep and mood, and reduces the discomforts that chemical changes in our bodies cause as we age. He uses the most up-to-date methods of dispensing Hormone Replacement Therapy (HRT), which avoid negative side effects and augments positive outcomes. In his practice of family medicine, Dr. Bukov understands that the way patients look can affect how they feel about themselves. He believes that retaining certain youthful qualities in our bodies help to retain an overall experience of well being.

A COMPASSIONATE DOCTOR UTILIZING LATEST SCIENTIFIC TECHNOLOGIES

Dr. Konstantin Bukov’s mission is to help his patients achieve their health and wellness goals. He is passionate about applying the latest aesthetic and anti-aging medicines. Dr. Bukov believes that we all need to preserve and restore the natural beauty that we were born with. His goal is to bring out the best in you, and to do so, he utilizes the latest science, technology, and state-of-the-art equipment.

What sets Dr. Bukov apart from other physicians is his ability to consider all facets of your health and wellness. Since his focus on aesthetic and anti-aging medicine will take your care to the next level, it will allow you to renew and restore your skin, your hormones, and even physical enjoyment. It takes the right mix of intellect, experience, and character to provide such well-rounded care, and Dr. Bukov does so in a relatable, friendly way.
