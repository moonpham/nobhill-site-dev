Forever bare BBL, laser hair removal


Previous Next

Laser Hair Removal can be ideal for clients wanting to reduce or eliminate time spent shaving, tweezing, or waxing. Lasers are useful for removing unwanted hair from the face, leg, arm, underarm, bikini line, and other areas.

Laser Hair Removal is one of the most commonly done cosmetic procedures in the U.S. During the procedure, laser or light energy is selectively absorbed by the melanin in hair follicles, effectively destroying the hair.

Typically, clients can achieve permanent hair reduction after an average of three to seven treatments. It takes multiple treatments because the multitude of targeted hair follicles will have differing active growth stages. The laser will not be effective on temporarily dormant hair follicles. Hair reduction is considered permanent when a significant amount of hair does not return for a period of time longer than the complete growth cycle. However, if hair does regrow, it will be finer and lighter than before.
