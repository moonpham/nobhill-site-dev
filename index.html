See a Slimmer You

CoolSculpting is a nonsurgical, fat-reduction treatment utilizing controlled cooling to eliminate stubborn fat which can be resistant to diet and exercise. CoolSculpting® is the only FDA-cleared cooling technology that targets and kills fat cells with no damage to the surrounding tissues. With more than three million treatments worldwide, it has become the world’s number one non-invasive, fat-reducing procedure. It is important to note that the CoolSculpting procedure is not a weight loss solution for people who are obese.

Feel younger looking skin

The Forever Young BroadBand Light (BBL™) Therapy is an innovative technology that sets new standards for skin conditions associated with aging, active life-styles, and sun damage. Forever Young Broadband Light (BBL™) Therapy keeps skin healthy and delays aging. BBL can effectively treat freckles, age spots, small blood vessels, acne, skin firmness, uneven skin texture, and unwanted hair. BBL treatments have quick recovery times leading to smoother, healthier, more vibrant skin, and an immediate change in skin texture and tightness.

Age healthier and live happier with bioTE

BioTE® Hormone Pellet Therapy uses hormones derived from natural plant sources to replicate the body’s normal hormonal levels. Implants, smaller than a grain of rice, are placed under the skin and consistently release small doses of hormones. For women, the Pellets contain a custom combination of estrogen and testosterone, whereas men’s Pellets are testosterone specific.
