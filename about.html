HIGHEST-TECHNOLOGY & NEWEST TECHNIQUES

We invest in the highest, latest technology and employs only non-surgical, non-invasive techniques. Our office indeed is proud to be the only few in the area who acquires the latest products in laser equipment, made right here in the Bay Area by Sciton, a Palo Alto company known worldwide as the maker of the best medical aesthetic lasers in the market.

These first-class devices ensure an in-patient experience that is quick, minimizing the discomfort in patients, and optimizing the best results. Diva™, The Vaginal Rejuvenation Device, for instance, is employed with High Precision Automation, takes approximately 3-5 minutes for the procedure. This equipment was carefully designed with patients’ comfort in mind. We are indeed proud to be the very first office in San Francisco to utilize this equipment on our patients.

In addition, to assure of purity and integrity of all injectables, we uses only highest quality FDA-approved products, purchased directly through US manufacturers.
